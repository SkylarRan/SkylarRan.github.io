叙述晚上的活动

Nov 12, 2019Hits

I got back home at 6 P.M. ,

到家（got home）

I got home late cause I’m working.

then I took off my work clothes/uniform.

and changed into some casual clothes.

When it went/got late,

天变黑了（get dark）

Your hair are starting to get grey.

我没注意天怎么变黑了（I have no idea how it got so late）

I pulled/closed the curtains/drapes.

pull ：拉上或关上

I felt a little hungry, so I wanted to order some food.

let me order you some food in a minute.

The delivery guy came.

外卖小哥

I checked the food and thanked him.

I finished the meal and wiped the table off.

you can finish everything , like food or work.

擦桌子 wipe the table off / wipe off the table

I relaxed for a while and read some book.

let’s relax for a second.

read-read [ri:d;red]

I picked up the remote control, and turned the TV on.

拿起（pick up）

遥控器（remote control / remote）

打开/关闭 turn on / turn off

turn the lights on

A talk show was on.

It’s 5 o’clock, my favorite TV show is on.

I used the remote to change channels.

During a commercial, I went to the kitchen for a snack.

电视广告（commercial）

常用的宣传广告（advertisement）

It was time to be/get ready for bed.

So I went upstairs, and took a bath.

上下楼 go upstairs / go downstairs

跑上楼 run upstairs

take a shower 淋浴

take a bath 泡澡

I used and flushed the toilet.

Oh, I just forgot to flush the toilet

After the bath, I put my pajamas on.

And I hung some of my clothes up in the closet.

挂 hang up

He hung up on me. 他挂了我的电话

After turning on my bedside lamp,

I switched/turned off the room light.

Then I pulled back the covers, and got into bed.

拉开 pull back

I lay down, set my alarm and fell asleep.
